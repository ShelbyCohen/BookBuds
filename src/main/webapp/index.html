About BookBuds

Discuss and learn with others reading the same book as you!

BookBuds is a web application that allows users to search for books and other media to interact with others. BookBuds goal is to have people engage and interact through online discussions among peers reading the same book.

When a user creates a BookBuds account, they have the ability to join a group based on a book title. Every group has its own community where they can post comments in an open discussion necessary to the topic. The user can share their knowledge and work from a material online such as an article or a pdf. There is also a messaging service where there can be peer-to-peer interaction directly.
